GLOSSARY ABOUT OTHER PROJECTS

Glossary

conformism
Tendency to conform to customs, to accept the ways of thinking or acting of the majority, the social norms.
set design
Art of representing in perspective. Art of designing stage sets; these sets themselves. All the pictorial, plastic and technical elements that allow the elaboration of a staging, notably theatrical, or of any show. Expressive arrangement of a complex architectural space.
naive architecture
Naive architecture or visionary environments refer to constructions built by self-taught people without artistic training.
