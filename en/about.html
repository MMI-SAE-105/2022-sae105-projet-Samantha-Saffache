About

This site was realized within the framework of a project in the MMI department of Montbéliard. The project "Notes for later" consisted in creating a site in which we have to present to our "us" of the future different elements such as a place, a book, a movie, a music, but also a regret and a hope of our generation.

My interpretation of the subject

That's how I wanted to create "Don't forget me", the name came to my mind because I would like to tell the future me not to forget who she was, and the dreams she had.

I would like the future me not to be afraid to be herself, without wanting to conform to the norms. That's why I decided, in my articles, to talk about self-esteem, conformity, stories of people who know how to be themselves, despite what others think, but also who realize their dreams.

I also speak about the fear of the others gaze, which is a brake in the acceptance of oneself, and the fact of erasing oneself, of not assuming who one is really. I also find that the various artistic forms allow to express oneself in a certain way, and to assert oneself.

So, the topics discussed encourage me to reach my goals, to learn about myself but also to want to be myself and not become someone I am not.

So I chose to create a site that brings together these works that have touched me and that inspire me.

Samantha Saffache student in MMI at the IUT Nord Franche-Comté
